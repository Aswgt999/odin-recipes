Swedish Meatballs

Description

Sunday suppers aren't compete without lasagna for many people coast to coast in America, and if the layered pasta dish isn't a tradition yet for your family, it will be after you serve this.

Ingredients(for 6 servings)

  • 1lb ground beef
  • 1lb ground pork
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 1/4 cups heavy cream
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of pepper
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon glour
  • 3/4 cup beef stock
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • salt
  • pepper
  • lingonberry jam(for serving)
  • mashed potato(for serving)

Steps

  1. Mix breadcrumbs, heavy cream, salt and pepper in a large bowl.
    Let it sit for approx 5 min for the breadcrumbs to soak in.
    Add mince dmeat and ceack in the egg.
    Mix with your hands until thoroughly combined.

  2. Roll mixtureint balls, slightly smaller than the size of ping pong balls.
    Add oil to a pan and fry meatballs until golden brown on all sides. approx 7-8 mins.

  3. Add butter into the pan and carefully shake the balls around to make them rounder. Transfer meatballs to a plate and set aside.

  4. Add flour to the pan and toast for a few minutes until golden brown.
    Add beef stock, remaining cream and soy sauce.
    Season to taste with salt and pepper. Simmer for a few minutes until thickened slightly.
    Serve meatballs with mashed potatoes, brown sauce and lingonberries.
